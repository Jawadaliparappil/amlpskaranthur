Admissions

Join Our School Family

We welcome applications from families who share our vision of quality education and holistic development. The admission process at AMLPS Karanthur is designed to be simple and transparent.

Admission Process:

  1. Fill out the application form (available online or at the school office)
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records if applicable)
  3. Confirmation of admission

For more information about the admission process, available seats, or to schedule a school visit, please contact our admissions office.
